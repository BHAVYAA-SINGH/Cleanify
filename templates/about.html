About Cleanify

Cleanify is a modern web application designed to streamline the process of reporting and managing maintenance issues within our campus community.

Our mission is to leverage technology to ensure a cleaner, safer, and more functional environment for everyone. By connecting requestees directly with the appropriate available workers through an automated system, we aim to reduce response times and improve accountability.

Our Goals

  • Provide an easy-to-use platform for reporting issues.
  • Automate task assignment for efficiency.
  • Ensure timely resolution of reported problems.
  • Facilitate feedback through a rating system.
  • Promote transparency and accountability.

We believe that a well-maintained campus contributes significantly to a positive learning and working atmosphere. Cleanify is our step towards achieving that goal together.
